Blog & FAQ

How to prepare for opening a load‑bearing wall?

Before touching a load‑bearing wall, call in a professional for a structural study. An architect or engineer will calculate the beam section required and assist with planning permissions. On site, careful shoring, precise cutting of the wall, installation of the IPN or HEB beam and meticulous finishing ensure a flawless result.

The cost of a concrete slab in Hérault

The price of a slab varies depending on the area, ground preparation and accessibility. Expect €70–€120 per square metre for a quality slab excluding earthworks. Reinforcement, concrete mix and drying time all influence the budget. Hiring a qualified mason ensures a flat, durable slab.

Renovating an old farmhouse: pitfalls and best practices

Renovating a farmhouse requires a complete diagnosis: roof structure, roof covering, stone walls, electrical and plumbing systems. The aim is to preserve authenticity while bringing everything up to standard. Call in specialists for each trade, plan the work step by step and allow for contingencies to stay on budget and schedule.

Choosing your mason in Hérault

To select a trusted mason, check their local references, verify their insurance (ten‑year and liability) and request several detailed quotes. A good professional will advise you on materials and techniques, comply with current standards and draw up a realistic schedule for your site.

Maintaining a wooden terrace

Regular maintenance is essential to prolong the life of a wooden terrace. Clean the boards with soapy water and a stiff brush, apply a saturating oil once a year and check the weatherproofing of fixings. Choose durable or treated species to withstand the Mediterranean climate.

Renovating your kitchen: steps and tips

To transform a kitchen, start by defining your needs: layout, storage and style. Call in a professional to prepare the site, replace services if necessary and install new units. Choose durable materials and optimise lighting for a functional, convivial space.
